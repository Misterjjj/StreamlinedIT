Security & Integrations

Open-standard hardware paired with unrestricted APIs for total visibility and control.

Why choose our stack?

  • True openness — direct API access to events and logs; no per-call fees.
  • Future-proof — UniFi & Square publish specs publicly; no surprise paywalls.
  • Ecosystem fit — Milesight cameras talk ONVIF to UniFi and push webhooks to Square for seamless clip-to-transaction workflows.

Typical build-out

  • 4K Milesight IP cameras with colour-night sensors
  • Ubiquiti UniFi Access for credentialled entry
  • Square POS for in-store transactions
  • Python or Node orchestration to correlate door events with sales/refunds

End result: unified audit trails, loss-prevention alerts, and total data ownership.
