  • Cloud: AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda), Microsoft Azure (VMs, Functions, Blob Storage), Google Cloud Platform
  • Networking & Security: Cisco & Ubiquiti routing/switching, firewall/IDS configuration, VPN architectures
  • Scripting & Automation: PowerShell, Python, Bash, Ansible, Terraform
  • Physical Security Integration: Milesight IP cameras, Ubiquiti UniFi Access, ONVIF, webhook-driven IoT workflows
  • Point-of-Sale & Analytics: Square POS APIs, data pipelines to SIEM and BI tools

Industry Experience

Our work spans retail, hospitality, healthcare, warehouses, and corporate offices. We’ve delivered solutions from single-site installs to multi-region rollouts with 24/7 monitoring and alerting.

Our Approach

We believe in open standards and full data ownership. Every integration is built on publicly-published APIs so your organization retains unfettered access to logs, events, and analytics—without unexpected fees or vendor lock-in.
